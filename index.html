About Us

NIVI PHOTOGRAPHY

Nivi Photography is a trusted photography studio based in Puducherry, known for its exceptional ability to capture life's most meaningful moments with artistry and heart. With a team that is professional, humble, and deeply passionate about their craft, Nivi Photography has built a stellar reputation for making every client feel comfortable and cherished throughout their shoot. From the golden streets of Kuyavarpalayam to grand wedding venues across the region, our lens tells stories that last a lifetime.

With a near-perfect rating of 4.9 out of 5 stars, our clients consistently praise our attention to detail, natural light photography, and seamless communication. Whether it is a grand wedding, an intimate engagement, or a special family occasion, Nivi Photography approaches every event with the same dedication and creativity.
